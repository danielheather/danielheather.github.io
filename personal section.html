More About Me

I am a Business Studies & Data Analytics graduate from Dublin City University. Since graduating I have gained experience across professional services at KPMG, large-scale event operations at the Convention Centre Dublin, and personal software development projects. I am driven by curiosity and a desire to keep learning, whether that means picking up a new programming language or diving into a new dataset.

To me, Data Analysis is another form of problem solving. The process of piecing together data insights into a clear picture of understanding is genuinely enjoyable. The learning curve of statistics, data science, and software engineering is steep, but the more I learn the more I enjoy it. Most recently I have been channelling that energy into iOS development, building a native wellness app using Swift and Apple's Screen Time APIs.

Outside of work, my interests include history, the UFC, fashion & design, and house music. I enjoy trying new things and having new experiences -- from live comedy shows to exploring Dublin's food scene. Guinness the cat continues to cost me a fortune at the vet, but he's worth every cent.
