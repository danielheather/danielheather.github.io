About me

I'm Daniel, I'm a final year Business Studies student, specialising in Business Analytics. PowerBI is my favorite programme. I love the ability that it gives me to tell the story of the data.

My interest in numbers began in my youth, drawn to the patterns in sports analytics and the problem-solving capabilities of mathematics. This passion steered me toward a Business Studies degree at Dublin City University, specialising in Business Analytics, where I'm on track to graduate with second-class honours.

My academic journey has been complemented by practical experience during my degree through the INTRA programme I was able to secure an internship at Kohlberg Kravis Roberts (KKR), delving into global market operations and financial analytics. This role allowed for refining of my analytical skills and developing a deep understanding of financial data's complexities.

Throughout my degree, I have been able to acquire over 50 analytics certifications from online learning platforms such as Kubicle and Datacamp. Becoming proficient in Python, SQL, R, and Microsoft Excel. My immediate goal following graduation is to enhance my analytical expertise through real-world experience and earning my CFA qualification.

In the long term, I aim to return to education for a master's degree, blending my professional experience with further study to enhance my financial and analytical skills. My journey is a testament to the power of continuous learning and the transformative potential of data.
