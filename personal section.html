More About Me

I am a 22-year-old undergraduate, currently in my final year of Business Studies at DCU. I have accounting and tax consulting experience, and chose Data Analytics in my final year because of my peronsal interest and to round out my skillset.

To me, Data Analysis is another form of problem solving. I find the process of piecing together data insights into a picture of understanding is very enjoyable. The Learning Curve of Statistics, Data Science and Software Engineering is steep, but with that I find that the more I learn the more I enjoy analytics. When it comes to projects like the design of this website, the journey has provided me satisfaction than the destination ever could.

Personally I enjoy trying new things and having new experiences. During this semester I took up running instead of going to the gym, I can confirm the runner's high is a real thing. I also started attending Live Comedy shows and was pleasantly surpised to discover the quality of the live scene in Dublin.

At Christmastime my neighbour knocked on the door and asked me if a kitten they had found was belonged to me, I said I would look after it for a few days. Since then Guinness has been costing me a fortune at the vet.
